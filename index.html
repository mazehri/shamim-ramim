Mission and vision of Gladwyn's Iron Company


SAFM’s vision is to be a successful, sustainable iron ore producer, with the potential to grow both organically and through acquisition,
and to be a company and partner of choice for both employees and local communities.
To achieve SAFM’s Vision, the Board of Directors and Management of SAFM will ensure that there is effective collaboration between all employees, contractors and consultants.
